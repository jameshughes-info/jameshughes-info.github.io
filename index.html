helping find & perform the perfect music for your event

Currently based in Warwick, I am available for events nearby

Please email contact@jameshughes.info to discuss how I might help

  • James is a music scholar at Warwick School, where he leads and plays in a wide range of groups & orchestras.
  • James studied clarinet & cello at the Royal Birmingham Conservatoire from 2016 to 2021, regularly performing with their Woodwind & String orchestras.
  • James performed, on the clarinet, with the National Children's Orchestra from 2017 to 2021.
  • James has performed alongside the Orchestra of the Swan, for some of their Warwick-based concerts.
  • James has performed alongside the English Symphony Orchestra, for some of their Malvern-based concerts.
  • James has performed with the National Youth Concert Band.
